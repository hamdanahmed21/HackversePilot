Project Atlas — Standing Decisions (as of Q3 kickoff)

1. The total Q3 budget for Project Atlas is capped at $50,000. Any spend above
   this requires sign-off from Finance before commitment.

2. All new user-facing features must pass an accessibility review before they
   are deployed to production. No exceptions for internal-only tools.

3. The team will not integrate any third-party analytics or tracking SDK into
   the product, per the privacy commitment made to early customers.

4. All marketing copy and campaign assets require Legal sign-off before they
   are published externally.

5. The production deployment freeze begins December 15th — no new releases
   after that date until January 5th.
